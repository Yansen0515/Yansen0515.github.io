Biography

I am an animal quantitative geneticist focusing on green and efficient livestock breeding. My work integrates advanced statistical genetics, molecular genomics and artificial intelligence to improve traits related to efficiency, sustainability and animal welfare. In particular, I study nitrogen use efficiency, methane emission genetics, health and welfare prediction, and AI‑assisted phenotyping to accelerate breeding progress.

我主要从事动物数量遗传学、绿色高效功能性状遗传改良、智能表型挖掘和动物大数据分析研究，重点关注氮利用效率、甲烷排放、健康福利和 AI 辅助育种。

Outside of research I enjoy exploring new computational techniques and collaborating with interdisciplinary teams to translate scientific discoveries into real‑world applications.
