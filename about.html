个人简介 / About

中文

陈焱森，博士，助理教授，学科专业为动物遗传育种。主要从事动物智能表型与精准育种研究，聚焦基于多组学与机器学习的复杂性状遗传解析与智能育种方法开发。

博士毕业于比利时列日大学，博士后继续在列日大学开展相关研究。曾两次获国家留学基金委资助赴比利时学习与科研，具有系统的国际化培养与研究经历。主持欧盟科研项目子课题2项，在 Journal of Dairy Science 等国际主流期刊发表多篇论文，多次在 EAAP 等国际学术会议作口头报告。担任 Animal、Genetics Selection Evolution、Journal of Dairy Science 等国际期刊审稿人。2025年入选湖南省第十七批“芙蓉计划”青年学者。

English

Yansen Chen is an assistant professor in Animal Genetics and Breeding at Hunan Agricultural University. His research focuses on AI-assisted phenotyping and precision breeding, with an emphasis on multi-omics- and machine-learning-based dissection of complex livestock traits.

He received his PhD from the University of Liège, Belgium, and continued postdoctoral research at the same university. His work spans nitrogen use efficiency, methane emissions, MIR-based phenomics, welfare and health traits, and genomic evaluation for sustainable livestock breeding.
