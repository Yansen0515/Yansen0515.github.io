Selected Projects

  • Nitrogen utilization efficiency in dairy cattle: Developing genomic evaluation methods and selection strategies to improve nitrogen use efficiency and reduce nitrogen excretion in dairy systems.
  • Genomic evaluation for methane efficiency in Walloon Holstein cattle: Integrating methane emissions data and genomic information to identify animals with naturally lower methane output.
  • Global health classification using MIR and machine learning: Building predictive models from mid‑infrared spectra to classify dairy cow health, fertility and metabolic status on a global scale.
  • Dynamic genetic regulation of nitrogen digestibility in Ningxiang pigs: Investigating gene expression and epigenetic regulation of nitrogen apparent digestibility in pigs to inform nutritional management and breeding.
  • AI‑assisted pig body weight and reproductive management system: Applying computer vision and AI to automatically estimate pig body weight, monitor reproductive parameters and support decision making in farm management.
