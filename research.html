Research Directions

My research integrates quantitative genetics, genomic evaluation, and machine learning to develop sustainable livestock systems. Below are some of the key directions I am actively exploring:

  • Genetic improvement of nitrogen use efficiency in livestock: identifying and selecting for genetic factors that enhance nitrogen utilization in dairy and pig systems, thereby reducing environmental footprint.
  • Genomic evaluation of methane emission traits: understanding the genetic architecture of methane production to breed animals with lower emissions without compromising productivity.
  • MIR‑based prediction of dairy cow health and welfare: using mid‑infrared spectroscopy data and machine learning to predict health, fertility and welfare traits in dairy cattle.
  • AI‑assisted phenotyping and precision pig breeding: developing advanced phenotyping pipelines and AI algorithms to measure growth, reproduction and welfare traits for precision breeding.

我当前研究的主要方向包括：
1. 提高反刍动物和猪的氮利用效率的遗传改良；
2. 甲烷排放特征的基因组评估；
3. 基于中红外光谱的奶牛健康福利预测；
4. AI 辅助的高通量表型采集与精准猪育种。
